Our Services

Laptop Sales

We offer brand-new and carefully refurbished laptops that meet strict quality standards. Each device is inspected by our technicians, fully cleaned, and tested. We provide honest descriptions of each unit, transparent pricing, and support to help you choose the right device for your needs — whether you're a student, gamer, or business professional.

Reselling (Top-brand Pre-owned Devices)

Our reselling service sources reputable, top-brand pre-owned devices that our team thoroughly inspects. We refurbish where necessary, replace worn components, and ensure each device operates reliably. You get access to high-quality products at reduced prices and a full overview of the device history and condition.

Repairs & Upgrades

From hardware replacements (screens, batteries, keyboards) to software diagnostics and driver updates, our technicians handle complex repairs with care. We also perform upgrades — SSD installations, RAM boosts, and thermal re-pastes — to extend device life and performance. Our repair work is documented and communicated clearly so you always know what we are doing and why.

Accessories Supply

We stock and source reliable accessories including chargers, docking stations, mice, keyboards, and premium laptop bags. All accessories are selected for durability and compatibility with popular laptop models.

Data Recovery

Lost files? Our secure data recovery process aims to retrieve important photos, documents, and business data. We follow industry-standard procedures to maximize recovery chances while protecting your privacy. We provide clear assessment and cost estimates before starting recovery work.

Software Installation & Optimization

We install and configure operating systems, productivity suites, antivirus solutions, and essential drivers. Our optimizations improve boot times, clean unwanted startup apps, and ensure your device runs smoothly and securely. We also offer post-installation guidance to help you maintain performance.

At Gadget Nexus we pride ourselves on a customer-first approach, transparent pricing, and technical excellence. Our goal is to keep your devices working, secure, and ready for whatever you need.
